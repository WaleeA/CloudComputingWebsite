The Future of Cloud Computing

Growth of Cloud Computing and its uses over the recent years

Currently cloud computing is seen as the practice of using a network of remote servers hosted on the Internet to store, manage, and process data, rather than a local server or a personal computer. The use of this system is growing exponentially as companies like Amazon and Microsoft have developed services like “Amazon Web Services” and “Microsoft Azure” for enterprises. Back in April 2012, it was reported that the Amazon servers held around 900 petabytes of data.

A key feature of cloud computing is its ability to give stronger performance for machine learning and Artificial intelligence. Machine learning is really about the study of algorithms that have the ability to learn through patterns and, based on that, make predictions against patterns of data. Google has intregrated it’s cloud services with its python enhanced software, tensorflow for the betterment of machine learning.
